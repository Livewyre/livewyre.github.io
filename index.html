Commission Information

All our quotes are based on purchase of miniatures, assembly, painting and delivery.

Our painting schemes are based on GW official art.

Our delivery estimate is 2-4 weeks after ordering to arrive at your door, we aim keep you updated with progress along the way to keep you informed on the various stages of the project.

Note: We do not charge anything more than the RRP you woud pay to purchase miniatures from a hobby store.

Assembly

All miniatures include:

  • Excess plastic removed
  • Mould lines cleaned
  • General sanding
Sub-Assemblies

Larger or Complex miniatures are also given sub-assemblies to get you the best paint finish.

Resin & Metal Miniatures

Resin & Metal models are additionally sanded and cleaned to remove excess parts and chemicals used during production.

Painting

Our miniatures are painted using:

  • Smooth Base Coats
  • Detailed Washes
  • Edge Highlighting
  • Targeted Dry Brushing
  • Wet Blending where appropriate
  • OSL where appropriate
  • Battle Damage & Weathering if applicapble
  • Free Hand where appropriate
  • Detailed Basing Materials
  • Varnishing to keep your miniatures looking great
Our detault paint schemes are based on GW official art, if you require something special please let us know, however this usually requires additional fees

FAQ

Unfortunatly we do not accept commissions using sent in miniatures. We have had issues in the past with damaged miniatures arriving or miniatures poorly assembled requiring more time to complete jobs. For this reason we prefer to buy the miniatures you desire and have them shipped right to us so we can get painting them right away and sent to you as quickly as possible!

If you desire to get miniatures painted from other ranges, aslong as they are freely available within the UK and fall under skirmish gaming, such as Infinity, Frostgrave, Malifaux etc. then we are happy to order them and paint them for you. Just be aware we are not responsible for stock shortages, increased delivery times to us, or cost of miniatures at time of order.
Unfortunatly due to the ammount of commision requests we recieve we are unable to offer full army painting as this requires a huge commitment of time and consistency from a single painter. For this reason we deal exclusivly with small skirmish teams, or individual skirmish units such as Forge World Blood Bowl/Necromunda specialist units.
Yes, we do offer custom paint schemes, but due to this not being our standard process it will come at an additional cost and time to produce. If you have a custom scheme already or wish to copy one you have seen other than GW, let us know in our contact form and link us to examples and we will do our best to produce it for you. We can also produce your own unique design based on a rough colour pallete or idea, just let us know and we can discuss it further.
Yes we can paint character models such as Skirmish team leaders, or a special unit such as Forge World Necromunda Hired Guns or Blood Bowl Star Players. We will make them stand out from your teams with extra highlighting, basing and painting techniques such as OSL. Costs for this will vary per commission, just send us an email below!
