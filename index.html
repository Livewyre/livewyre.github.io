Livewire Paints

We are a small miniature painting studio based in the UK. We have been painting miniatures for more than 15 years at a variety of levels.

Our team specalizes in painting Games Workshop/Forge World, specifically small skirmish teams or individual characters. Due to the amount of commissions we do, we do not offer services for mass painting full Warhammer armies.

About Our Commision Services

Quality Painting

All our miniatures are painted to an above tabletop standard, including features such as OSL, Wet Blending and High Quality Basing

Large Range

We can paint skirmish and character units from: 40k, AoS, Necromunda, Warcry, Kill Team, Blood Bowl & Pretty much anything sold by Games Workshop or Forge World

Simplified Process

When you place an order, we purchase the minis for you, paint them, keep you updated along the way and ship them to you as fast as we can

Passion

All our painters are passionate about miniature painting and we hope our work shows it

Note: If you want something outside of the GW/FW range, and it is readily available such as Infinity or Star Wars Legion, then get in touch and we may be able to provide you with a custom quote for the work.

Commision Guide

All our quotes are based on purchase of miniatures, assembly, painting and delivery.

Most of our commissions take between 2-4 weeks after ordering to arrive at your door, but we keep you updated with progress along the way.

Note: We do not charge anything more than the RRP you woud pay to purchase miniatures.

Assembly

All miniatures include:

  • Excess plastic removed
  • Mould lines cleaned
  • General sanding

Larger or Complex miniatures are also given sub-assemblies to get you the best paint quality.

Resin & Metal models are additionally sanded and cleaned to remove excess parts and chemicals used during production.

Painting

Our miniatures are painted using:

  • Smooth Base Coats
  • Detailed Washes
  • Edge Highlighting
  • Targeted Dry Brushing
  • Wet Blending where appropriate
  • OSL where appropriate
  • Battle Damage & Weathering if applicapble
  • Free Hand where appropriate
  • Detailed Basing Materials
  • Varnishing to keep your miniatures looking great

FAQ

Unfortunatly we do not accept commissions using sent in miniatures. We have had issues in the past with damaged miniatures arriving or miniatures poorly assembled requiring more time to complete jobs. For this reason we prefer to buy the miniatures you desire and have them shipped right to us so we can get painting them right away and sent to you as quickly as possible!

If you desire to get miniatures painted from other ranges, aslong as they are freely available within the UK, such as Infinity, Star Wars Legion, War Path or Board Games such as Kingdom Death Monster, Gloomhaven or D&D then we are happy to order them and paint them for you. Just be aware we are not responsible for stock shortages, increased delivery times to us, or cost of miniatures at time of order.
Unfortunatly due to the ammount of commision requests we recieve we are unable to offer full army painting as this requires a huge commitment of time and consistency from a single painter. For this reason we deal exclusivly with small skirmish teams (5-10 units), Character models like Elites/HQ Units and Boardgames with a few minis such as Gloomhaven or Kingdom Death Monster, or specialist 1 off models for display.
